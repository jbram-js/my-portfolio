About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Joe Bramwell, I am 25 years of age and live in Ramsbottom just outside of Manchester. I work at Bolster Systems - a construction documentation system - where my main role is focused around user experience, technical support and brand development. When I am not working you can find me taking on graphic design and motion graphics freelance projects, or more recently, learning to code with Manchester Codes!

My main hobbies are focuessed around sport, music & food. I am an avid Burnley FC fan and have been a season ticket holder for 20 years, following home and away most weekends. I enjoy going to gigs and live music events. Most gigs I attend are Indie Rock/Pop, Lo-Fi or Hip-Hop. I worked for 5 years at the UK's largest festivals as a chef for various catering companies which has allowed me to build up quite a portfolio of acts watched live! I love all things food! Trying a new restaurant, drinking a new Gin or IPA or cooking a new recipe at home. You would most often find me socialising with good friends at local bars or restaurants at the weekend! I enjoy exercising (as much as anyone can possibly enjoy it!). I am the player/manager of a Sunday league team in Ramsbottom and enjoy going to the gym, out on runs or long country walks with the dogs! I try my best to travel as much as possible and have speant time in New Zealand, South East Asia, Sri Lanka and mainland Europe. Hoi An, Queenstown & San Sebastian are some of my favourite places visited.
